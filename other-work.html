Standing Room Only

Stanford-Lipsey Newsroom, 2018

I spent some time in college frantically trying to get Bob Boilen's attention. And after roping together a ragtag team of audio engineers and videographers, much trial and error and some atrocious lighting, we nailed it: Standing Room Only, our local recreation of NPR's infamous Tiny Desk. It held strong long enough for us to make some pretty fun stuff. That is until a group of editors politely asked that I remove the punk bands from the newsroom.
