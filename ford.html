Northeast Ohio Dealers, Summer 2021 Campaign

In May 2021, dealerships across the country were pulling their ad spend due to the great semi-conducter chip shortage. But our clients in Northeast Ohio understood the power of brand equity. So we pitched them a campaign that put a spin on the traditional walkaround video starring their spokesman Chris Agos to keep the Ford brand top of mind, even when supplies were short.
